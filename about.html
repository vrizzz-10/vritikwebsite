ABOUT US

The Sports Club Program at Inderprastha Engineering College is comprised of sports clubs formed,developed,governed and administered by the student membership working in conjuction with the office of Intramurals and Recreation, a unit of the Division of Student life. The key to the success of this program is student leadership, interest, involvement and participation.The sports club council with advisement of the office of Intramurals and Recreation has created this manual in order to provide offficers with the structural framework necessory for clubs to gain recognition,develop leadership skills, and run safe, efficient programs. The policies and procedures contained herein apply to all of the clubs. Sports club members, officers,coaches, and the faculty/staff advisor are required to read, understand and follow the procedures outlined in this manual. Questions regarding the policies and procedures within this manual should be directed to the Director of Intramurals and Recreation or the Sports Club Council executive board.


Purpose of the Sports Club Program

The sports Club program at Inderprastha Engineering College offers the the community an opportunity to participate in a broad variety of sports and recreational activities . All programs are based on student interest and designed to enhance the vertos experience by creating an environment where students can unite in diverse groups to achieve common goals and objectives while encouraging healthy lifestyles . Sports Club provides a valuable learning experience through student involvement in fundraising ,public relation,organization, administration, budgeting, scheduling ,teaching and leardership development . The clubs with the most success and stability are those with active members and dedicated officers .


Definition of a Sports Club

A Sports Club is defined as a registered student organisation that exist to promote and develop interest ina particular sport or physical acivity . A club's focus may be recreational, instructional, competitive, or a combination of these types of activities based on its constituion . Sports Club are stricty voluntary and involvement in a Sport Club should enhance the student's college experience and contribute to the student's overall education and wellness.


Role of Directors and Intramurals and Recreation

The Director of Intramural and Recreation serves primarily as an advisor and resource person for Sports Club leaders and is available to assist student representatives with club business. It is the Director's responsibility to monitor club activities to ensure all college procedures are being followed ,to assist clubs in implementing sound safety practices, to assist club in managing club funds in accordance with college official policy, to facilitate club activities and to ensure the policies and procedures are followed. The Direcor will allow clubs as much freedom to operate as possible, provided they operate within the regulations and guidelines herein. The director shall recommend decisions to the sports club Council on item not specifically covered in this manual.


Sports Club Council

The Sports Clubs Council(SCC) is the primary, student-lead oversight coommittee responsible for advising, advocating and unifying all organizations within the Sports Club Program at Inderprastha Engineeering College and ensuring that participants are aware of their rights and responsibilities at Inderprastha Engineeering College Sports Club Student athletes. The SCC will serve as the link between Spots Club and the college administration, and be advised by the Director of Intramurals and Recreation. The SCC shall set and enforce the Sports Club policies and procedures.


         Composition

  • A represetative from each recognized sports club, who is in good academic and social standing at Inderprastha Engineeering College.
  • These members having voting rights and may represent their club on the Sports Club Council.
  • The Director of Intramurals and Recreation who will not have voting rights.
  • The chairperson does not have voting rights, except to break a tie.

         Responsibilities of Club

  • Follow the SCC constitution and by-laws.
  • Conduct an SCC meeting atleast once per month.
  • Assist the Director of Intramurals and Recreation in conducting leadership training for sports club officers.
  • Recommend revision of Sports Club Manual and procedures for the following year.
  • Review and approve clubs seeking new admittance and/or reinstatement into program.
  • College wide representation of the sports clus program at events, program,etc.
  • All SCC recommendations and/or actions are subject to approval or rejection by the Director of Intamurals and Recreation.
